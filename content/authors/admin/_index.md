## About Me
I am a Ph.D. candidate at the Institute of Energy, Environment and Economy, Tsinghua University, and my advisor is Da Zhang (http://www.energyda.cn). My research interests includes ultra-high resolution power system optimization modeling, multiple energy resource assessment, optimization theory, and the application of artificial intellegence in management sciences. I have developed the Energy-Resource Assessment Model (E-RAM), Renewable Energy Siting and Power-system Optimization Model (RESPO, https://github.com/mrziheng/RESPO), Integrated Sustainable Power-system Optimization Model series (including CISPO for China and GISPO for the whole world). I graguated from Renmin University of China (https://www.ruc.edu.cn) and recieved bachelor degrees (mathematics and computer science) from School of Information (http://info.ruc.edu.cn).
